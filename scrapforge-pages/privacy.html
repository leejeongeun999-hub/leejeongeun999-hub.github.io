Privacy Policy — ScrapForge

Last updated: July 22, 2026

ScrapForge works without an account or developer-operated server. The developer does not collect data from the app.

Data stored on your device

All game progress stays on your device, including your hero's gold, gear, mastery, contracts, achievements, and expedition progress. This information is used only to save and restore your game.

Backup and deletion

If you export a backup, ScrapForge creates a file and hands it to your device's normal share sheet. The developer does not receive the file. You can remove local data by deleting the app.

No advertising or tracking

ScrapForge does not include advertising or an advertising SDK. It does not request tracking permission, access device identifiers for tracking, or show a custom tracking-consent prompt.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the developer using the contact information on the App Store product page.
