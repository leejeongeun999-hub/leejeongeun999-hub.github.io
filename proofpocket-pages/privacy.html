ProofPocket Privacy Policy

ProofPocket stores the proofs and deadlines you add on your iPhone. It does not require an account or operate its own cloud service.

When you choose a receipt image, text recognition runs on your device to suggest a merchant, date, and amount. Your selected image is not sent to ProofPocket’s servers.

ProofPocket uses Google Mobile Ads. Where required, Google’s consent flow is shown before ads start. Advertising providers may process data as described in the App Store privacy label and their own policies.
