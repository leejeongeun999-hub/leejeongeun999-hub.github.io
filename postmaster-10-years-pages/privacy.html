Postmaster: 10 Years — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 22, 2026

No data collected

Postmaster: 10 Years does not require an account and does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. The app contains no advertising or analytics SDK.

Local game data

Your game progress and language preference are stored only on your device. The developer cannot access this local data. Removing the app removes its local game data, subject to normal device backup behavior controlled by Apple and the user.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect information from children or any other users.

Changes and contact

If this policy changes, the effective date will be updated. Privacy questions can be sent through the contact address on the app's App Store page.
