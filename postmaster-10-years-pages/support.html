Postmaster: 10 Years — Support

Before contacting support

Try closing and reopening the app. Game progress is saved locally after each settled quarter. The app does not use an account or cloud-sync service.

Contact

For support, bug reports, or accessibility feedback, use the developer contact address shown on the app's App Store page. Include your iPhone model, iOS version, app version, and the steps that caused the problem. Do not include passwords or sensitive personal information.
