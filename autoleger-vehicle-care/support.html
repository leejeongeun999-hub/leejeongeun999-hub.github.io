AutoLedger Vehicle Care Support

Manage multiple vehicles, maintenance schedules, ownership records, local documents, exports, backups, and reminders entirely on your device.

Need help?

Email leejeongeun999@gmail.com with your iOS version and a short description of the issue. Do not send VINs, registration numbers, insurance documents, or other sensitive vehicle information.

Data recovery

The app has no account or server backup. Use the in-app backup feature regularly and keep exported backup files in a safe location you control.

Maintenance notice

Intervals shown by the app are planning references. Always follow your official owner's manual and manufacturer instructions.
