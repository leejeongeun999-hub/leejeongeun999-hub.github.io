Selah — Support

Contact

For questions, bug reports, or feedback about Selah, please reach out via the contact details listed on the app's App Store page, or open an issue on the project's source repository.

Common questions

Do I need an account? No — Selah works fully anonymously with no sign-in.

Where is my data stored? Entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to a server. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How do I back up my data? Settings → Back up now creates a file you can save anywhere via your device's share sheet.

How do I delete my data? Settings → Delete my data permanently erases everything stored on-device.

I'm struggling and need help right now. Open the Me tab → "Need to talk to someone?" for crisis support hotline numbers.
