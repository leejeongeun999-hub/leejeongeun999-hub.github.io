Privacy

CouponPocket processes coupon screenshots selected by you on your device. Coupon names, expiry dates, and barcode text stay in local app storage unless you choose to remove them.

The app uses Google Mobile Ads. Where required, it asks for consent before ads start and provides ad privacy options in Settings.

CouponPocket does not require an account and does not run its own server.
