Privacy Policy

Unfold · Last updated: July 13, 2026

Unfold is a self-reflection app that shows one question per day and lets you write a personal answer.

Data we collect

Unfold does not collect, store, or transmit your journal entries. Entries remain on your device unless you choose to delete the app.

Advertising

Unfold displays Google AdMob advertising after an entry is saved. Google may process advertising identifiers, IP address and general location, device and operating-system details, and app interaction data to provide advertising and measurement.

You can manage tracking permission in iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. For Google's practices, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Children

Unfold is not directed to children under 13.
