Oathbound Arena Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Overview

Oathbound Arena is an offline single-player hero training RPG. The app does not require an account, does not run its own gameplay server, and does not collect game progress on a developer server.

Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores roster progress, battle progress, settings, and optional backup data on your device. If you export a backup, your device share sheet handles where that file goes.

Advertising

The app is free and supported by Google AdMob ads. Google may process device identifiers, approximate location, ad interaction data, diagnostics, and related data to provide ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and comply with law. In regions where consent is required, the app shows Google's consent form before requesting ads. On iOS, the app may also ask Apple's tracking permission before personalized advertising.

You can change eligible ad privacy choices inside the app through the ad privacy options button.

No Sensitive Features

The app has no user-generated content, chat, account login, payments, health tracking, or precise location features.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Jeongeun Lee at leejeongeun999@gmail.com.
