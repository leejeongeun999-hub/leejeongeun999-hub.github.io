PackTrail Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

English

PackTrail is an offline travel packing planner. The app does not require an account, does not run a server, and does not collect trips, packing items, notes, baggage limits, or backup text on our own servers.

The app does not include ads, third-party tracking, analytics SDKs, account login, or in-app purchases.

Travel packing data is stored locally on your device. If you use the backup text feature, you choose where to keep that backup.
