DayForge Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

DayForge is a local daily planner. The missions, time blocks, habits, focus sessions, and reviews you enter are stored on your device unless you export a backup yourself.

Data you enter

DayForge does not operate an account system, server profile, calendar-account connection, AI service, or public sharing feed. The app does not ask for account passwords.

Backups

If you use the export feature, iOS opens a share sheet so you can choose where to save the backup file. Imported backups are processed on device.

Advertising

DayForge uses Google AdMob to show ads. Google may process data such as advertising identifiers, approximate location, ad interactions, diagnostics, product interactions, crash data, and performance data for advertising and app functionality. In regions where consent is required, DayForge shows Google's consent flow before requesting ads and provides ad privacy options when required.
