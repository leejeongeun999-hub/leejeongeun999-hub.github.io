PackCue Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026

Overview

PackCue helps you create packing and departure checklists. The app is local-first: trip names, checklist items, and checked states are stored on your device. PackCue does not require an account and does not operate a PackCue server.

Information You Add

You may enter a trip name or destination and checklist changes. This information stays on your device unless you choose to share it through your device features.

Advertising

PackCue is a free app supported by ads. We use Google AdMob to show ads. Google and its partners may collect or receive information from your device to provide, measure, and improve ads, subject to your consent choices and applicable law. You can review Google's advertising and privacy practices at Google Privacy Policy.

Consent Choices

Where required, PackCue uses Google's User Messaging Platform to request and manage ad consent. You can open "Ad privacy options" in the app settings to review available choices.

Analytics

PackCue does not include a separate analytics SDK in this release.

Children

PackCue is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children.
