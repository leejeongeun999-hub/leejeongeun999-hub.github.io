Abyssforge Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Overview

Abyssforge is an offline single-player action RPG. The app does not require an account, does not run a game server, and does not collect gameplay data on our own servers.

Local Data

Hero progress, inventory, settings, and backup data are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, local data may be deleted by iOS.

Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to show ads. Google may process advertising data, device identifiers, approximate location, product interaction, crash data, and performance data according to your consent choices and platform settings.

Consent Choices

Where required, the app shows Google's consent form before requesting ads. You can update ad privacy choices from the app settings where available.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact leejeongeun999@gmail.com.

개요

Abyssforge는 오프라인 싱글플레이 액션 RPG입니다. 계정 가입, 게임 서버, 자체 서버 기반 플레이 데이터 수집을 사용하지 않습니다.
